# Design System Specification: Architectural Ledger

## 1. Overview & Creative North Star
**The Creative North Star: "The Digital Blueprint"**

This design system is not a collection of templates; it is a structural philosophy. Inspired by the precision of architectural drafting and the depth of midnight sky charting, it moves beyond standard UI by treating the screen as a three-dimensional ledger.

The aesthetic is driven by **Intentional Asymmetry** and **Tonal Depth**. We reject the "boxed-in" look of traditional dashboards. Instead, we use expansive breathing room, overlapping structural elements, and a high-contrast typography scale to create a sense of authoritative luxury. In Dark Mode, the interface should feel like a high-end command center-precise, emissive, and deeply layered.

## 2. Colors
- Primary: #85adff (container #6e9fff)
- Surface base: #060e20
- Surface container: #0f1930
- Surface container highest: #192540
- Tertiary accent: #61c2ff

### No-Line Rule
Use tonal shifts and spacing gaps instead of 1px section dividers.

### Glass & Gradient Rule
Floating elements use translucent surface + blur. Primary CTAs use a 135deg gradient from primary to primary container.

## 3. Typography
Inter is used across display/headline/title/body/label scales for editorial clarity.

## 4. Elevation & Depth
Use tonal layering first; shadows are rare, soft, and low-opacity.

## 5. Components
Buttons, inputs, cards, chips, and list structures prioritize spacing and tonal hierarchy over borders.

## 6. Do and Don't
Favor asymmetry, spacing, and subtle contrast; avoid hard borders, pure white text, and playful full-round shapes except status indicators.
